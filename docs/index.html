My Story

A Musical Journey

From a child humming tunes to a stage-ready performer — here's how music became my life.

2015

The First Note

At age 7, I sang my first solo at a school function and discovered my love for music. From that moment, there was no looking back.

2017

Classical Training Begins

Began formal Carnatic music training under a renowned guru, Master Abishek. Mastered swaras, ragas and developed a powerful vocal range that would became my signature.

2019

First Competition Win

Won the State Level Youth Singing Championship — a turning point that boosted confidence and opened doors to larger stages and audiences.

2021

First Studio Recording

Recorded my debut single in a professional studio, blending classical elements with contemporary music. The song gained widespread appreciation online.

2023

Rising Performer

Featured in multiple cultural festivals and music concerts across the state. Recognised as one of the most promising young voices in the region.

2025

New Horizons

Expanding into fusion music, collaborating with artists from diverse backgrounds and preparing for my first full-length album release.
